MINISTRIES KIDS

GROWING KIDS IN CHRIST

SUNDAYS AT 9:00AM & 10:30AM

Our Ministries Kids ministry includes kids ages 8wks-5th Grade. Because we believe that Scripture is clear that parents are the
primary disciple-makers in the lives of their children, our hope is to partner with parents in their pursuit of this mission, coming
alongside them in the discipleship process. Our desire is to provide excellent kid’s programming in a safe and secure environment
where kids can learn about God’s love, build friendships and have a whole lot of fun along the way!

Babies

THE GARDEN & THE SANDBOX:
8 WEEKS TO 2 YEARS

In our nurseries we strive to create a safe and clean environment for babies to enjoy. Parents can check their children into the nursery and then enjoy going to the service and building relationships. We also offer a cry room off the hallway of our worship center where moms can enjoy the live sermon broadcast and take care of their children.

Elementary

THE CLUBHOUSE: 3 YEARS – 5 YEARS

In our Preschool ministry, we work to create the first building blocks of a strong, Christ-centered foundation for our children. We teach from the Scriptures and help parents proclaim to their kids who God is and what He has done in Jesus Christ. During the weekends we sing, make crafts and teach the Bible to the kids.
