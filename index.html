YOU’RE WELCOME HERE!

SERVICE TIMES: SUNDAYS AT 9:00AM & 10:30AM
LOCATION: MADINA ESTATE 14TH STREET KPAKU BOYS

At Christ Ministries, you’ll be welcomed into a friendly, casual environment by people who are excited to see you. Our
Sunday services have great music, a message from one of our pastors, fun and safe kids programming, and much
more! We’d love to meet you, so come as you are and find out more for yourself by joining us this Sunday at Central!

WHAT TO EXPECT

  • Each service to last just a little over an hour.
  • Thought-provoking and engaging messages based on the Bible.
  • Teaching from one of our Central Pastors.
  • High-energy worship music.
  • Fun and engaging teaching for your kids.

    • FOR THE KIDS

    Each weekend Central Kids, newborns through 5th graders, learn the truth about who God is and who they are because of Him through age- specific lessons, small groups, and trusted adult leadership. Our desire is to provide excellent kid’s programing in a safe and secure environment where kids can build meaningful friendships and have a whole lot of fun along the way!

    LEARN MORE

    FOR THE TEENS

    Each week Central Students meet on Wednesdays for dinner at 5pm for all, Middle School at 6pm, and High School at 6:30pm. Loaded with great music, interactive activities, and small group time it addresses real issues teens face each day. Our major push for the middle & high school years is to equip our students to face the challenges that will come their way, as they venture out into a world that will test their faith.

    OUR VISION

    They are what help guide our actions and help us stay on target to what God is doing through His church.
